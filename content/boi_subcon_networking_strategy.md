# BOI / SUBCON Thailand 2026 Networking Strategy  
## Phuket Health Rehabilitation Center / Medical Center Project

**Project context:**  
We are developing a **Phuket-based Health Rehabilitation Center / Medical Center** project, intended to qualify under Thailand BOI’s **Health Rehabilitation Center / Medical Service** framework.

Key project strengths:

- **Land already secured / controlled**
- **Prime Phuket commercial location**
- Nearby **mall**
- Nearby **beach and port**
- Highly accessible, walkable area
- Located in a commercial zone where patients, families, staff, and partners can easily access services
- Approximate project budget: **200 million THB**
- Intended positioning: **medical-grade rehabilitation center**, not a hotel or general wellness resort

---

# 1. Main Objective of Visiting SUBCON Thailand 2026

The goal is not just to walk around the exhibition.

The goal is to leave with:

1. **10–15 qualified supplier / partner leads**
2. **3–5 budget quotations**
3. **2–3 possible operating / JV / medical technology contacts**
4. Clear BOI linkage contacts
5. Evidence that can support the BOI application
6. Better understanding of medical technology, rehab equipment, digital systems, and healthcare facility infrastructure available in Thailand

The key strategy is to use SUBCON as a **BOI credibility-building exercise**.

We want to show that our project is:

> A real medical rehabilitation investment with secured land, strong budget, medical technology, continuous treatment programs, supplier network, and long-term economic value to Thailand.

---

# 2. Core Positioning Message

Use this wording consistently:

> We are developing a **200 million THB Phuket Health Rehabilitation Center** under a BOI medical service structure. The project is located on already-secured land in a highly accessible commercial area near a mall, beach, and port. We are looking for medical technology suppliers, rehabilitation equipment, healthcare facility systems, digital patient-tracking solutions, automation partners, and potential JV or operating partners.

Avoid saying:

- “Wellness resort”
- “Hotel with medical services”
- “Spa retreat”
- “Luxury villa project”
- “Health hotel”

Better wording:

- Health rehabilitation center
- Medical recovery center
- Rehabilitation treatment programs
- Overnight rehabilitation care
- Medical technology
- Patient monitoring
- Physiotherapy and recovery programs
- Clinical / medical service infrastructure
- Phuket medical tourism support

The BOI risk is that the project may be seen as a hotel or wellness property. The language and actual structure must show that the main activity is **medical rehabilitation**, with accommodation only supporting **overnight treatment**.

# 3. Why SUBCON 2026 Is Relevant

SUBCON Thailand 2026 is useful because BOI is linking the event to **Health & Wellness**, medical devices, suppliers, technology, automation, and business matching.

The BOI/SUBCON health and wellness presentation highlights these healthcare growth drivers:

- Medical tourism
- Aging population and non-communicable diseases
- Demand for wellness services
- National healthcare insurance and private insurance growth
- Growing middle class
- Digital adoption by healthcare providers
- High-value healthcare treatments
- Supporting regulatory landscape

These are directly relevant to the Phuket project.

Your project should be framed as supporting Thailand’s healthcare growth story:

> Phuket already has strong tourism infrastructure. Our project upgrades the destination into higher-value medical recovery and rehabilitation services, supporting medical tourism, senior rehabilitation, post-treatment recovery, and health-tech adoption.

---

# 4. BOI Angle to Keep in Mind

The BOI medical service incentive slide shows **Health Rehabilitation Center** under **Group B**.

This is important because Group B may not provide a corporate income tax holiday, but can still provide valuable non-tax incentives such as:

- Permission for foreign ownership
- Permission to own land
- Visa and work permit facilitation for foreign experts
- Permission to remit foreign currency abroad
- BOI credibility and investment facilitation

So the internal argument should not be:

> We are doing BOI mainly for tax exemption.

It should be:

> We are doing BOI to create a clean foreign investment structure, support land/project ownership, bring in foreign medical expertise, attract partners, and position the project as a serious medical investment.

# 12. How to Avoid Looking Like a Hotel Project

This is critical for BOI.

## Do

Say:

- Overnight treatment rooms
- Patient rooms
- Recovery accommodation
- Medical rehabilitation programs
- Treatment schedule
- Therapy rooms
- Rehab technology
- Clinical supervision
- Patient monitoring
- Medical referral network
- Nursing / physiotherapy support

## Do Not Lead With

Avoid leading with:

- Hotel rooms
- Resort
- Villa rental
- Spa
- Lifestyle retreat
- Luxury accommodation
- Tourism revenue
- Airbnb / OTA sales
- Restaurant income
- Beach holiday

## Structural Recommendation

Separate revenue categories:

| Category | BOI Treatment |
|---|---|
| Rehabilitation program fees | Core BOI activity |
| Medical / therapy services | Core BOI activity |
| Overnight patient accommodation | Supporting BOI activity |
| Restaurant / café | Separate / supporting, not main |
| Spa / massage | Separate non-core activity |
| General hotel stays | Avoid or separate clearly |
| Retail / wellness products | Separate non-core activity |

Accommodation should be described as necessary because the BOI category requires **continuous rehabilitation programs including overnight treatment**.

The project budget is strong because the basic Health Rehabilitation Center investment threshold is much lower than 200M THB.

Possible high-level allocation:

| Budget Area | Approx. Allocation |
|---|---:|
| Building / renovation / construction | 80M–100M THB |

| Digital health / IT / patient tracking | 5M–10M THB |

Important: land value and working capital may not count toward the BOI minimum investment requirement, so the BOI consultant should structure the calculation carefully.

# 14. How the Phuket Location Helps the BOI Story

The land location is a major strength, but it must be framed correctly.

Do not frame it only as:

> Good for tourists and commercial rental.

Frame it as:

> Good for patient access, medical tourism, family support, staff recruitment, supplier access, emergency referral, international patient comfort, and rehabilitation lifestyle compliance.

Location advantages:

- Easy patient access
- Good for foreign patients and families
- Close to lifestyle infrastructure
- Walkable environment supports rehabilitation
- Nearby commercial services support long-stay recovery
- Beach / port proximity supports Phuket’s medical tourism appeal
- Commercial area improves staffing and supplier access
- Strong visibility for a medical recovery brand
- Suitable for outpatient and inpatient-style overnight rehab programs

Strong wording:

> The location supports rehabilitation outcomes because patients can stay in a controlled medical recovery environment while still having access to safe walking routes, nearby services, family amenities, and Phuket’s international tourism infrastructure.
