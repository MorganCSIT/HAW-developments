Partner narrative

A real medical rehabilitation investment needs real operating capability.

The project is being structured around medical service credibility: rehabilitation equipment, patient monitoring, digital health systems, facility infrastructure, healthcare operators, training partners, and suppliers with Thailand or Phuket service capability.

What we need

Supplier and partner categories.

Rehabilitation equipment

Physiotherapy, gait training, balance systems, mobility equipment, hydrotherapy support, and therapy documentation tools.

Digital health systems

Patient tracking, EMR/HIS, scheduling, outcomes dashboards, equipment maintenance logs, and patient CRM.

Medical operators

Clinical governance, medical director networks, therapy protocols, staffing models, training, and launch support.

Facility infrastructure

Accessible patient rooms, nurse-call systems, infection control, pharmacy fit-out, emergency readiness, and Phuket servicing.

BOI planning file

Useful supplier evidence makes the project stronger.

We are looking for partners who can provide practical documents for planning: company profile, product catalogue, budgetary quotation, certifications, Thailand service capability, and notes on healthcare project experience.

  • Medical device and rehab equipment catalogues
  • Digital health and patient monitoring specifications
  • Budgetary quotations for planning
  • Installation and Phuket service capability
  • Operating, JV, or demonstration-site interest
