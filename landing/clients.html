Patient story

From hospital discharge to daily-life confidence.

A typical patient arrives after surgery, injury, illness, or functional decline. The center is designed to combine physician review, therapy planning, supervised sessions, overnight recovery rooms, progress tracking, and discharge planning in one controlled environment.

01

Review

Medical intake, goals, risk screening, and therapy plan.

Recover

Progress tracking, mobility checks, and patient education.

04

Return

Discharge planning, home guidance, and follow-up options.

Packages

Choose the recovery pathway that fits the need.

Post-surgery recovery

For patients after orthopedic, spine, fracture, ligament, or medical procedures who need multiday recovery support.

Sports injury rehabilitation

For athletes and active adults rebuilding range of motion, strength, balance, and return-to-play confidence.

Senior mobility rehab

For older adults needing gait training, fall-prevention support, transfer practice, and supervised strengthening.

Chronic pain / lifestyle rehabilitation

For spine, musculoskeletal pain, deconditioning, and lifestyle-related recovery goals that need structured coaching.

Preventive health medical program

For people seeking screening-led planning, movement assessment, nutrition review, and health-risk reduction.

Overnight recovery

Patient rooms support treatment, rest, and monitoring.

Accommodation is planned as part of the recovery model: accessible rooms, nurse-call readiness, therapy scheduling, medication routines, and patient-tracking systems that keep the program organized.

  • Accessible recovery rooms
  • Program tracking and progress notes
  • Therapy and nursing coordination
  • Family-friendly Phuket location
