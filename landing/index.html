Project snapshot

A medical recovery center, not a hotel concept.

The center is being structured around continuous rehabilitation programs, inpatient-style patient rooms, medical technology, therapy spaces, clinical governance, and supplier partnerships that support a credible BOI medical-service position.

Programs

Five focused packages for patients and referral partners.

Post-surgery recovery

Structured support after orthopedic, spine, fracture, ligament, or general medical procedures.

Sports injury rehabilitation

Progressive therapy for athletes and active adults rebuilding strength, balance, and confidence.

Senior mobility rehab

Fall-prevention, gait training, strength work, and supervised mobility support for older adults.

Chronic pain / lifestyle

Multiday rehabilitation for spine, musculoskeletal pain, deconditioning, and lifestyle change.

Preventive health medical

Screening-led programs combining medical review, movement assessment, nutrition, and monitoring.

Clinical identity

Built around treatment pathways and measurable progress.

Patients move through physician review, therapy planning, supervised sessions, outcome tracking, nursing support where needed, and discharge planning. Accommodation supports the recovery program; it is not the product.

  • Therapy documentation and outcomes tracking
  • Patient monitoring and nurse-call readiness
  • Rehabilitation equipment and accessible rooms
  • Supplier records suitable for BOI planning files
